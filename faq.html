Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does Aqua Guest ensure the well-being of the betta fish during events?

At Aqua Guest, the health and well-being of our betta fish are our top priorities. Each fish is placed in a spacious bowl with live plants and a gentle light source, creating a comfortable and serene environment. Our experienced team handles the transportation, setup, and care of the fish to ensure they thrive throughout your event.

2. What is included in the pricing for the betta fish centerpiece package?

The pricing for our betta fish centerpiece package includes the cost per bowl, a service fee per night, and a delivery fee calculated based on the event location. Each bowl comes with a betta fish, live plants, and a gentle light source. Our team takes care of the setup, transportation, and return of the fish after the event.

3. Can I choose specific betta fish for my event?

Yes! You can specify which color betta you would like at each table. You can also specify which decorations you would like in each bowl. Examples include hearts, letters, castles, photos and so forth.

4. What happens to the betta fish after the event?

After your event, the betta fish will be carefully transported back to our facility, where they will continue to receive proper care and attention. We are committed to the well-being of our fish, and our team ensures a smooth transition for them after each event.

How It Works

Step 1: Choose Base Bowl Size and Count

Select the base bowl size that fits your event's aesthetic. You can also choose the quantity of bowls needed for your tables.

Step 2: Choose Sand Color

Customize the look of your centerpiece by selecting the sand color. Choose between classic black or natural for a more organic feel.

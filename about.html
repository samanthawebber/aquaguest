About Aqua Guest

Aqua Guest offers a unique and humane alternative to traditional event centerpieces.


We specialize in renting betta fish as centerpieces for weddings, conventions, and parties. We understand the importance of creating a memorable and visually stunning atmosphere for your guests. Our centerpiece solution not only adds a touch of elegance but also promotes the well-being of our aquatic friends.


Our bettas come in spacious bowls adorned with lush plants and soft lighting, creating a serene and captivating display for your tables. We prioritize the comfort, happiness, and safety of our fish, ensuring they receive the care they deserve throughout your event. After the celebration, the bettas return to our facility, where they continue to thrive under our dedicated care.


What sets Aqua Guest apart is our commitment to providing a humane and beautiful alternative to traditional table decorations. With Aqua Guest, you can enjoy a stunning centerpiece that not only enhances the ambiance but also promotes the ethical treatment of animals.


Thank you for considering Aqua Guest for your special event. We look forward to adding a touch of aquatic magic to your celebration while making a positive impact on the well-being of our finned friends.
